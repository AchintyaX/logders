Cancellation Policy

In case of change / cancellation in bookings you need to inform us at least 30 days in advance.
Cancellation in case less than 30 days and more than 15 days of the date of stay only 50% will be refunded.
Cancellation of booking less 15 days no refund is applicable.
After check-in, no refund is applicable in any case.

REFUNDS

On receipt of your cancellation of bookings via E-mail we will initiate a refund to your credit card.You will receive the credit within a certain amount of days, depending on your card issuer's policies.

NO SHOW

  • In case of No Show no refund is applicable.
