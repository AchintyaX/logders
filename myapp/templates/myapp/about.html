About Us

Corporate Stay

As a corporate, we understand the importance of stay of your employees to be pleasing and comfortable. Finding a suitable location with proximity to work and at the same time provide quality accommodation and safety could be an inundating task. At the Lodgers, we own the responsibility to ensure that an employee or a guest gets a comfortable close to home experience. Our serviced apartments are located near the major business hub in Gurgaon. These include Studio Apartments, 1 Bed Service Apartments and Twin Bedded Service Apartments. Serviced by our professional Support Team, they have become a preferred choice of stay by executives from large international, national and multinational companies. Our clients make up a wide range of industries, products, and services.

Here are the salient features of our Corporate Program:

  • Self-contained rental apartments.
  • Fully furnished Homes with WiFi & TV targeting mid to senior level executives and their families.
  • Ideally designed for longer stays of more than a month to a year.
  • Layout of every apartment organized to ensure that it is possible to work, cook/eat and sleep in each self-contained unit, irrespective of the size.
  • 24 hour attendant.
  • Fully equipped Kitchen.
  • In-apartment facilities of self-laundry via fully automatic washing machine.
  • Wardrobe / storage.

We keep in mind to:

  • Get feedback of your guests and keep reinventing as per their needs.
  • Corporate who ensures 100 room nights a year qualifies to get our special corporate rates.
