Know About Us

BirdsandBees (BNB)  Serviced Apartments in Gurgaon are ideal for people travelling on business trips, medical guests, a family for vacation and even for wedding guests. The spacious rooms and elegant décor of our serviced apartments provide a perfect stay for visitors seeking the best in an affordable price range. Our reliable security system ensures to keep you safe and secure and our courteous staff, the finest hospitality.

As a corporate, we understand the importance of stay of your employees to be pleasing and comfortable. Finding a suitable location with proximity to work and at the same time provide quality accommodation and safety could be an inundating task. At Birds and Bees, we own the responsibility to ensure that an employee or a guest gets a comfortable close to home experience.

Our Facilities

The facilities that you will avail are:

Wi-Fi Internet

We provide unlimited access to the internet round the clock. Our internet facility is reliable and you can avail to execute your business work.

Kitchen

We offer individual Kitchens in all units with complete cooking facilities so that our guests can whip up a meal just the way they would do it back in their own home and kitchen.

Air-conditioning

Our rooms are perfectly air-conditioned so that you can stay in luxury. We have power backup, in the case of a power cut, your life will be untouched.

Laundry

We provide you with a washing machine for self washing. Alternatively, you can call us for laundry. We will dry-clean your clothes by professionals and our charges are reasonable.

Credit/Debit Card Accepted

We accept all kind of credit/debit cards for payment; moreover, we also accept payment through net banking.

Newspapers

We provide prominent newspaper daily.

Airport pickup and drop on demand

You can also ask for airport transfer.

Doctor on call

In the case of emergency, we also facilitate our customers 24 hrs medical assistance, so you can carefree enjoy your staying.
